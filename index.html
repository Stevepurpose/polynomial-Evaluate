Polynomial Evaluator

Example:2x4+x2+1

Method

  • Enter co-efficients of x from largest degree to the lowest degree(power of zero) in the first input box.
  • Enter 2 as coefficient of x4
  • Enter 0 as coeffiecient of x3 since it is not present
  • Enter 1 as coefficient of x2
  • Enter 0 as cofficient of x, since it is not present
  • Enter the last as 1. Since it is a coefficient of x to the power 0.
  • Click on enter coefficient after each coefficient entry.
  • Enter value of x when you are done entering coefficients.
  • Click on sum polynomial after entering value of x.
  • Click on display to view coefficients.

Note: Enter 0 as coefficient for degrees not present after highest degree.
