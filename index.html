Polynomial Evaluator

Enter co-efficients of x from largest degree to lowest degree(power of zero) in first box

Note:enter 0 as coefficient for degrees not present.
Example:2x^4+x^2+1

METHOD

  • Enter 2 as coefficient of x^4
  • Enter 0 as coeffiecient of x^3 since it is not present
  • Enter 1 as coefficient of x^2
  • Enter the last as 1 as it is a coefficient of x to power 0.
  • 2x^4 + 0*(x^3)+1*(x^2)+0*(x^1)+1*(x^0)
  • Click on enter coefficient after each entry and enter value of x in next box.
  • Click on sum polynomial when all is done.
  • Click on display to view coefficients.
